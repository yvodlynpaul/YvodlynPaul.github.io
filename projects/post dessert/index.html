About

Post Dessert is a trendy and innovative dessert shop located in the heart of downtown. The store's signature dessert is a decadent chocolate lava cake, made with the finest imported Belgian chocolate and served warm with a scoop of hand-churned vanilla ice cream.

The store's sleek and modern interior features minimalist decor with pops of bright pink and gold accents, creating a chic and Instagram-worthy backdrop for your sweet treats. And if you're looking for something to wash it all down, Post Dessert also offers a selection of coffee and tea beverages, including cold brew coffee, matcha lattes, and herbal teas.
